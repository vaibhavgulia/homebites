Instant Ramen Upgrade

Preparation Time: 10 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Instant ramen packet
  • Egg
  • Chilli sauce
  • Spring onions

Steps

  1. Boil noodles as per instructions
  2. Add seasoning
  3. Crack egg into boiling ramen
  4. Top with chilli sauce and spring onions
