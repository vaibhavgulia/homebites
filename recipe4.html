Maggi Masala Twist

Preparation Time: 8 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Maggi noodles
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Masala packet

Steps

  1. Saute onion and tomato
  2. Add water and bring to boil
  3. Add Maggi and masala
  4. Cook until soft and serve hot
