Dalgona Coffee

Preparation Time: 5 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp instant coffee
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp hot water
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix coffee, sugar, and hot water
  2. Whip until thick and creamy
  3. Pour cold milk in a glass
  4. Add whipped coffee on top
