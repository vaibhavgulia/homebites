Chocolate Mug Cake

Preparation Time: 5 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a mug
  2. Stir until smooth
  3. Microwave for 1-2 minutes
  4. Enjoy directly from mug
